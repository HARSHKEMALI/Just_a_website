Origin Story of Atria University

Like all superheroes, universities also have an origin story. Atria University's story begins in the vibrant streets of Bengaluru, a city known for its constant flux and innovation. Born in India's global center of high technology, Atria University has always upheld technological advancements and environmental transformation as its greatest motivator1.

Vision and Approach

Atria University aims to address the challenges of tomorrow by preparing students for careers and opportunities in the future. Here are some key points about Atria University:

  1. Unique Model: Atria University is creating a new pedagogy and methodology for education. It endows students with skills of eternal value, empowering them to meet the challenges of tomorrow.
  2. Curriculum Design: The university curates a curriculum with experiential learning and interdisciplinary thinking at its core. Experts from industry and academia worldwide contribute to shaping this curriculum.
  3. Flexible Learning Pathways: Students can discover their interests and passions before choosing a specific degree. Atria University offers flexible learning pathways.
  4. Project-Based Appraisals: Instead of traditional exams, students undergo project-based evaluations reviewed by faculty and peers.
  5. Sprint-Style Delivery: Practical knowledge is imparted through 3-week workshops, allowing students to build solutions.
  6. Holistic Admissions Process: Atria University sees students as more than just test scores during the admissions process.

Leadership

  • Vice Chancellor: Dr. Rajeevan Madhavan Nair, a distinguished Climate Scientist with over 38 years of research experience in weather and climate science, leads Atria University. His illustrious career includes significant contributions to prestigious institutions and impactful roles in government service2.
  • Advisory Board: Eminent leaders from academia serve on the Advisory Board, shaping the university's path towards excellence..
